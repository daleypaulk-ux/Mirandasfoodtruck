Our Story

A Family Tradition, Served Fresh Daily

Miranda's Mexican Food Truck isn't just a place to eat — it's a celebration of Mexican culinary heritage, brought to the streets of Kansas City by Ana and Luis with passion, care, and family recipes passed down through generations.

Parked permanently at 3029 Southwest Blvd, we've built a community around our truck. Every taco, every huarache, every quesadilla is made with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love. Come hungry. Leave happy.

We also offer birria on select Fridays — follow us for announcements. Ask about off-menu veggie tacos too!
