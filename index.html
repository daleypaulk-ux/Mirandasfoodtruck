Our Story

Family Owned and Operated, Always Fresh

Miranda's Mexican Food Truck is a family owned and operated kitchen on wheels where the Mexican flavor is handmade food and genuine hospitality. Come join us in the heart of Kansas City.

Voted Best Restaurant in KC 2025 — recognized on BusinessRate for outstanding local dining and customer experience.

Located at 3029 Southwest Blvd, we deliver a warm, welcoming experience year-round with a cozy patio and fresh food made to order every day. Stop by and taste the difference.

Our menu is handcrafted daily with quality ingredients, including locally sourced produce whenever available.

Hosting a celebration, office lunch, or community event? We offer catering, event service, and easy delivery on DoorDash and Uber Eats, so enjoying Miranda's is always convenient.
